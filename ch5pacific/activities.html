Activities at Pacfific Trails

Hiking

Pacfific Trails Resport has 5 miles of hiking trails and is adjacent to a state park. Go it alone or join one of our guided hikes.

Kayaking

Ocean kayaks are available for guest use.

Bird Watching

While anytime is a good time for a bird watching at Pacific Trails, we offer guided birdwatching trips at sunrise several times a week.
